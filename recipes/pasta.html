Creamy Tuna Pasta Salad

Tuna salad meets pasta salad in this light and creamy mash-up that's the perfect side for a picnic or cookout.

Ingredients:

  • Dried farfalle pasta
  • Canned tuna
  • Shallots
  • Celery
  • Fresh dill
  • Frozen peas
  • Mayonnaise
  • Plain yogurt
  • Dijon mustard
  • Lemon

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta
  2. Make dressing
  3. Prep vegetables and tuna
  4. Drain pasta
  5. Stir together
